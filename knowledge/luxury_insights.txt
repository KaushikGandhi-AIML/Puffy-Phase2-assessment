Title: Luxury Buyer Psychology — Sleep Category

High-income US consumers ($100K+ household income) value:
- Quality over price
- Craftsmanship
- Self-care & wellness
- Longevity / anti-aging
- Quiet luxury aesthetic
- Brand integrity
- “Smart” purchasing decisions
- Minimalist, modern design
- Unique experiences

Marketing that works well:
- Cinematic visuals
- Sensory-rich storytelling
- Subtle, intelligent humor
- Artsy, design-forward framing
- Emotional depth
- "Invisible luxury" aesthetic
- High-tech meets high-comfort messaging

Do NOT use:
- Price drops
- Discounts
- Loud, cheap visuals
- "Act now!" marketing tones
