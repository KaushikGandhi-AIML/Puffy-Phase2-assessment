Puffy — Company Introduction

Puffy is a U.S.-based direct-to-consumer sleep brand that specializes in luxury mattresses, bedding, and bedroom accessories. Founded on the idea of creating products that feel like “sleeping on a cloud,” Puffy blends advanced foam engineering, design, and customer-centric service to deliver a high-end sleep experience.

**Core Mission & Vision**  
- To develop total bedroom solutions for optimal sleep health across North America. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}  
- To provide comfort, quality, and safety in their products, combining craftsmanship with wellness and long-term value. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}  
- To support sustainability and community: Puffy runs a Mattress Donation Program, donating gently used or returned mattresses to shelters and reducing waste. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}  

**Product Range**  
- **Memory Foams & Hybrid Mattresses**: Puffy offers a variety of mattress models, including premium memory foam designs and hybrid models with contour-adapt coils for greater support and stability. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}  
- **Bedding & Accessories**: Beyond mattresses, Puffy also sells luxury bed sheets, body pillows, and blankets — all designed to match the brand’s comfort-first philosophy. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}  
- **Quality & Safety**: Puffy mattresses use CertiPUR-US® certified foam, with no fiberglass fire-retardant layers, ensuring safety, low emissions, and high durability. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}  

**Customer Experience**  
- Puffy provides a **101-night risk-free trial**, allowing customers to test their product before fully committing. :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}  
- They back their mattresses with a **lifetime warranty** — a strong signal of confidence in their product’s durability. :contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}  
- The company sells primarily online (DTC), but has expanded through partnerships with brick-and-mortar retailers, making their products accessible both digitally and in physical stores. :contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}  

**Certifications & Manufacturing**

- They operate manufacturing facilities in the U.S., emphasizing control over quality and supply chain. :contentReference[oaicite:10]{index=10}  

**Community & Social Impact**

- Puffy is widely recognized in the luxury mattress space, known for its “cloud-like” comfort and premium build quality.  
- Their core audience includes design-conscious and wellness-oriented buyers who value premium comfort, long-term sleep investment, and elegant bedroom aesthetics.
